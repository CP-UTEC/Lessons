Competitive Programming Club UTEC (CPC-UTEC)

Competitive Programming Club UTEC (CPC-UTEC) is an organization at UTEC. You can found us on Facebook or you can contact us writing to cpc@utc.edu.pe.

If you want to join the group the process of admission can be found here. The admission contest are held in the first weeks of every semester.
